About ACAP-Bicol

Last updated: 3/3/2026

1. Project Overview

The Agro-Climatic Advisory Portal (ACAP) - Bicol is a specialized digital platform designed to bridge the gap between climate science and practical agriculture. Developed through a collaborative effort, the portal serves as a central hub for climate-resilient agricultural advisories tailored specifically for the Bicol region.

Our platform integrates real-time weather data from PAGASA with crop-specific recommendations to help farmers make informed decisions in the face of a changing climate.

2. Mission and Vision

Mission: To empower the Bicol agricultural community with precise, timely, and actionable climate information to mitigate risks, improve crop productivity, and ensure food security.

Vision: To be the leading regional provider of innovative agro-climatic services, fostering a resilient and sustainable agricultural sector in Bicol.

3. Core Objectives

The portal aims to achieve the following goals:

  • Provide localized 10-day and seasonal weather forecasts.

  • Deliver crop-specific advisories based on current and predicted weather conditions.

  • Facilitate the dissemination of emergency weather alerts via SMS and web notifications.

  • Support agricultural extension workers and policy makers with data-driven insights.

  • Promote climate-resilient farming practices through accessible digital tools.

4. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding the project, its data sources, or collaboration opportunities, please reach out to our team at the Department of Agriculture Regional Field Office 5 - AMIA Program, San Agustin, Pili, Camarines Sur.
